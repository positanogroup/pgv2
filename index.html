Why Positano Group?

How we help you make sense of things

Financial Visibility & Planning

We build the "plumbing." From unit economics to rolling forecasts, we give you a clear, simple view of your cash and performance. We ensure you have the visibility needed to grow without losing control of your burn or operations.

Strategic Decision Support

We act as a partner to help you navigate your most important choices. Whether you're deciding how to allocate capital, hire, or pivot, we provide a financial lens to ensure you're making the best decisions possible for your long-term value.

Transaction & Capital Readiness

We don't act as brokers; we act as your internal finance engine. We professionalize your data room and sharpen your financial narrative so that when you sit down with banks, VCs, or PE firms, you are coming from a position of strength.

High Standards and Hands-On Execution

Our work is informed by over 15 years of experience at world-class firms and high-growth businesses. We bring the rigor of McKinsey and Evercore together with the hands-on grit of a startup and private equity portfolio company operator.
